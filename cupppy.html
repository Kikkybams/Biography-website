Dj

Florence Ifeoluwa Otedola (born November 11, 1992), professionally known as DJ Cuppy, or simply Cuppy, is a Nigerian disc jockey and producer. She is the daughter of a renowned Nigerian businessman Femi Otedola. She grew up in Lagos and moved to London at the age of 13.

Early life and education

Cuppy lived in Ilupeju for six years before moving to Ikeja where she attended Grange School, Ikeja. She then relocated to London for her GCSEs and A-Levels. She graduated from King's College London in July 2014, with a degree in business and economics. She also earned a master's degree in music business from New York University in 2015.[5][6] As from October 2021, Cuppy is enrolled and pursuing a master's degree in African Studies at the University of Oxford.

Philanthropy

In July 2018, Cuppy launched the Cuppy Foundation. The following year (November 2019), the foundation organized the Gold Gala and raised over N5bn for the Save the Children initiative Nigeria. Nigerian business Dangote and her father donated to the cause.
